About Us

The Country Information Portal is your go-to destination for discovering information about countries around the world. We provide comprehensive details about various aspects of each country, including geography, culture, economy, history, and more.

Our team of experts curates and verifies the information to ensure accuracy and reliability. Whether you're a student researching for a project, a traveler planning your next adventure, or simply curious about the world, our portal has something for everyone.

Countries and Capitals

  • Country: United States
    Capital: Washington D.C.

    The United States of America (USA) is a vast country located in North America, known for its diverse culture, bustling cities, and stunning natural landscapes.

  • Country: United Kingdom
    Capital: London

    The United Kingdom (UK) consists of four constituent countries: England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. It has a rich history and cultural heritage.

  • Country: France
    Capital: Paris

    France is famous for its art, architecture, cuisine, and fashion. Paris, the capital city, is known as the "City of Light" and is a major global center for culture and commerce.
